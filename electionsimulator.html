Election Simulator

This is a simulator meant to simulate a hypothetical election with Ranked Choice Instant Runoff Voting. Press R to reset the election

Parties

There are four parties: Labor, Liberal, Social, Conservative, Libertarian, Green, Reactionary, and Communist

Labor

A moderate left party most similar to other labor parties

Liberal

A moderate right party most similar to Democratic Party in the United States

Social

A left party that advocates for social democracy

Conservative

A right party most similar to the Republican Party in the United States

Libertarian

A party advocating for the lessening of the size of government and a more free market

Green

A party that advocates for the protection of the environment, aswell as social programs

Reactionary

A far-right party advocating for an strong and authoritarian nationalist state.

Communist

A far-left party advocating for an authoritarian state with a state-owned economy.
